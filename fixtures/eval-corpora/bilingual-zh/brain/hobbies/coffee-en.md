# Coffee

Morning ritual, non-negotiable: one V60 pour-over, no phone allowed until the cup is finished.

Current recipe: 15g beans, 250g water at 92°C, 45-second bloom, total brew time around 2:45. Grinder is a Comandante C40 at 24 clicks. Kettle is a Fellow Stagg EKG.

Beans come from Nylon Coffee Roasters in Everton Park — Ethiopian naturals are the default. I buy 250g every second Saturday and the queue is part of the ritual. Lazy backup: Common Man blend.

Failed experiments: cold brew (too much planning), an espresso machine (returned within a month — the counter is too small), flavoured beans (never again).

Office coffee is strictly for emergencies.
