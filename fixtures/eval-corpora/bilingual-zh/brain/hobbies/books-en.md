# Book club

We meet the first Sunday of each month, 4pm, at a corner table in Tiong Bahru Bakery — six regulars, a two-book buffer rule, and whoever picks hosts.

June 2026 pick: "The Three-Body Problem" by Liu Cixin (刘慈欣). I read it in the original Chinese years ago; reading the English translation now is a strange mirror experience — Ken Liu's footnotes explain things I never thought needed explaining.

All-time favourites from this club: "Klara and the Sun" (Ishiguro), "Project Hail Mary" (Weir), "Invisible Cities" (Calvino). The one everyone hated except me: "Piranesi".

My pick is scheduled for September 2026 and I'm going with "The Remains of the Day".
