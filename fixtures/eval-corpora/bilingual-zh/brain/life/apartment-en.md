# Tiong Bahru apartment

Two-bedroom walk-up on Eng Watt Street, third floor, rented since August 2022. Rent is SGD 2,800 a month. Landlord is Mr. Lim, who lives two blocks away and fixes things suspiciously fast — the water heater died on a Sunday, and a new one was installed by Monday 9am.

The lease renews every March; the 2026 renewal went up SGD 150 and Mr. Lim apologised about it twice.

Things I love: the pre-war floor tiles, the morning light in the piano corner, walking distance to the wet market. Things I tolerate: no lift, the karaoke uncle on Friday nights.

Aircon servicing every quarter (Cool Serve, SGD 90 for both units). The balcony rattan chair officially belongs to the cat.
