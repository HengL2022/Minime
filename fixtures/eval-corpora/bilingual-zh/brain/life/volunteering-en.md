# Volunteering

One Saturday a month at Willing Hearts soup kitchen in Kembangan — the vegetable-chopping shift, 6:30am to 10am. Started in 2023 after a colleague dragged me along once; the auntie brigade at the chopping station now saves me a spot and judges my knife skills slightly less than they used to.

Also signed up as an occasional translator (English ↔ Mandarin/Cantonese) for a migrant workers' clinic run by HealthServe; roughly one Sunday evening every two months, mostly helping older Cantonese-speaking uncles explain their symptoms to doctors.

Rule I set for myself: volunteering does not get cancelled for work. It has survived three "urgent" product launches so far.
