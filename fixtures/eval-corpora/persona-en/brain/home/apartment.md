# Apartment on Bakklandet

I rent a two-room apartment at Nedre Bakklandet 23B, third floor, in one of the old wooden
houses with a view over the Nidelva river and Gamle Bybro. Moved in August 2021.

Rent is 14,500 NOK per month including heating, paid on the 1st. Landlord is Odd-Einar
Strand, who lives on the ground floor, fixes things within a day, and leaves bags of plums
on my doormat every September. The lease renews yearly in June; he's hinted he won't raise
the rent as long as I keep helping him with his iPad.

Quirks: the floor slopes enough that a dropped apple rolls to the kitchen, the stairs are
death-narrow (the sofa came in through the window via a crane, 2,300 NOK well spent), and
in January the wind off the fjord finds every gap in the 1890s walls. Biscuit gets the spot
by the radiator.

Internet: fiber, 500/500. Power is on a spot-price contract with Tibber. The storage unit
in the cellar holds the bike, winter tires, and the camping gear.
