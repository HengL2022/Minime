# About me

I'm Priya Raghunathan, 34 years old, born on 14 March 1992 in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India.
I moved to Norway in 2017 for a master's degree in marine technology at NTNU and never left.
These days I live in Trondheim and work as a senior acoustic systems engineer at Fjordsonics AS.

Languages: Tamil (native), English (fluent), Norwegian (B2, working on it), and embarrassingly
rusty Hindi. I think in English, count in Tamil, and swear in Norwegian.

I share my apartment with Biscuit, my whippet. My closest family is far away — my parents are
still in Coimbatore and my sister Meera lives in Toronto — so video calls on Sunday evenings
are sacred.

Three things that keep me sane: long runs along the Nidelva river, playing the nyckelharpa
badly but enthusiastically, and bouldering on Wednesdays. I am the kind of person who keeps a
spreadsheet for everything, including a ranked list of every cinnamon bun bakery in Trondheim
(current leader: Sellanraa).
