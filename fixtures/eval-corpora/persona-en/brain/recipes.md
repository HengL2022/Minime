# Recipes and cooking

## The canon

- **Paati's rasam** — my grandmother's recipe, the one thing I make when homesick. Secret
  is roasting the rasam powder fresh and a thumb of crushed ginger at the end, off heat.
  Tamarind from the Asian shop on Olav Tryggvasons gate.
- **Skillingsboller, cardamom-heavy** — my Norwegian-Indian fusion flex: triple the
  cardamom, brown butter in the filling. Took 11 attempts to stop them unrolling in the
  oven (the fix: seam down, rest 20 minutes before baking).
- **Sunday dal** — toor dal, pressure cooker, eaten after long runs.

## Sourdough

My starter is named **Gunnar**, born January 2023, 80% hydration, rye-fed, lives in the
fridge and gets fed Fridays. Gunnar has survived two vacations (Ingrid babysits him) and
produces a weekly Saturday loaf. Best result: 30% wholegrain spelt with seeds.

## Rules of the kitchen

Everything gets cut with the Sakai Takayuki santoku from Japan and nothing else is allowed
near it (it's carbon steel — wipe and oil after use). No hazelnuts cross the threshold,
obviously. The spice shelf stays in Amma's frequency-of-use order.
