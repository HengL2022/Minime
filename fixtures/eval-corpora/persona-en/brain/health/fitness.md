# Running and fitness

Big goal: the Trondheim half-marathon on 13 September 2026, target time under 2 hours.

## Training plan

I run three times a week: intervals on Tuesdays, easy run on Thursdays, long run on Sundays.
Long runs go along the Nidelva river path and up to Lade. Current weekly volume is about
32 km, building toward 40 km by August.

## Personal bests

- 5K: 24:58 (parkrun, October 2025)
- 10K: 52:41 (Trondheim løpskarneval, May 2026)
- Half-marathon: never raced one before — September will be my first.

## Body maintenance

Right knee acts up past 15 km — physiotherapist Kari Nystrøm at Rosenborg Fysio diagnosed it
as runner's knee (patellofemoral pain) and gave me a strength routine: single-leg squats,
hip abductions, and step-downs, three rounds twice a week. It's working; the knee survived
an 18 km long run in May without complaints.

Shoes: Saucony Ride 17, bought new pair April 2026, rotating with old pair. Replace around
700 km.
