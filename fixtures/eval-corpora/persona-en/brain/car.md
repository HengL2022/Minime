# Car — Škoda Octavia

2019 Škoda Octavia station wagon, petrol, bought used in 2022 with 61,000 km on it.
Registration plate VH 52301. Now at about 98,000 km. Mostly it exists for mountain trips,
dog-to-vet logistics, and the monthly big shop; in town I bike.

- Service: Møller Bil Trondheim, full service done March 2026 (oil, filters, brake fluid).
- Next EU-kontroll (periodic inspection) due June 2027.
- Winter tires: Nokian Hakkapeliitta 10, studded, on separate rims in the cellar. Shift
  around mid-October and back after Easter.
- Insurance: If Skadeforsikring, ~6,200 NOK/year with bonus.

Known issues: a slow puncture in the rear left that Dekkmann couldn't find (probably the
valve — watch it), and the infotainment screen reboots itself when it's below minus ten,
which the dealer says is "normal for the model year," which is a lie I've chosen to accept.

The dream replacement is an electric Octavia-sized wagon once the cabin fund is done, but
realistically this car has five more years in it.
