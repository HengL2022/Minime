# Bouldering

Wednesday evenings are bouldering at Trondheim Klatresenter with Ingrid — it's how we met
in 2019, when she lent me chalk and then sandbagged me onto a 6A I had no business trying.

Current level: comfortable on 6B, occasional 6B+ on a good day, project grade 6C. Slab is
my strength (runner legs), overhangs are my doom (no upper body to speak of). Shoes are
La Sportiva Solutions, downsized one size, which Ingrid says is cowardly.

We do an outdoor trip once a summer — last year was Flatanger, where I top-roped and mostly
photographed Ingrid doing actual hard things. This summer we're aiming for Hell (the climbing
crag, though the jokes never stop) in July.

Finger care: I tape my left ring finger after a pulley tweak in March 2026. Hangboarding is
banned until it's been pain-free for two months.
