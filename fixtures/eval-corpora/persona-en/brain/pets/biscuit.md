# Biscuit the whippet

Biscuit is my whippet, born 3 May 2022, from a breeder in Steinkjer. Fawn coat with a white
chest blaze. Fastest creature I have ever seen and also the laziest — 30 seconds of zoomies,
23 hours of sleeping on the radiator-side end of the sofa.

## Practical

- Vet: Dr. Annika Moe at Heimdal Smådyrklinikk. Annual vaccination due each February.
- Food: Acana Adult Small Breed, 90 g twice a day, plus an indefensible quantity of dried
  fish skin treats.
- Chip number is in the vault doc. Insured with Gjensidige.

## Quirks

Terrified of thunderstorms and of the robot vacuum (which we therefore only run when she's
out). Pulls hard toward the fjord on walks because seagulls exist. Has a knitted wool jumper
(mustard yellow) for anything below minus five; whippets have no insulation whatsoever.

Walks: short loop along Bakklandet in the morning, big one to Festningen after work. On
Sundays she comes along for the first 3 km of my long run, then gets dropped home.
