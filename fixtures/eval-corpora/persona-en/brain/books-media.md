# Books and media

## Book club

First Monday of every month, 19:00, upstairs at Antikvariatet on Bakklandet. Six regulars.
I joined in 2022 via Ingrid. June 2026 book: "The Overstory" by Richard Powers — I'm 200
pages in and already planning to make everyone listen to facts about mycorrhizal networks.

## All-time favorites

- "The God of Small Things" — Arundhati Roy is my favorite author; I reread it every couple
  of years and it's different every time.
- "The Sea Around Us" by Rachel Carson — the book that probably aimed me at marine
  acoustics without my noticing.
- "Project Hail Mary" — engineer comfort food.

## Listening

Podcasts on runs: "99% Invisible" and "In Our Time". Music while working: Väsen (nyckelharpa
propaganda), Nils Frahm, and a lot of Carnatic violin (L. Subramaniam) which is Appa's
influence. The record player gets Saturday mornings — currently a lot of Alice Coltrane.

## Watching

Biscuit watches the nature documentaries, but only if there are birds.
